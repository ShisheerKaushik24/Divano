About Me

I graduated from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art with the Class of 2020, earning a Bachelor of Engineering in electrical engineering and majoring in computer engineering. My coursework involved extensive work in both hardware design and software development. I've taken courses in software engineering, data structures and algorithms, web development, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, operating systems, and computer architecture.

I have experience working in a variety of technologies, from developing full-stack web and Android apps, to programming Raspberry Pi's for IoT devices, to building machine learning models in TensorFlow. I've worked in teams of up to 10 people, and contributed towards nonprofit projects intended to aid people in need, both within my home city of New York and third-world countries.

I have a passion for learning new technologies and polishing my craft, and aspire to use my skills to help others all throughout the world. My dream is to travel around the world, visiting cities in Europe and Asia and experiencing new cultures, all the while building applications that can help solve real-world problems people are facing. My hobbies outside of professional work include reading science fiction and fantasy, weightlifting, cooking, playing chess, and gaming.
